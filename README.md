## What is Free Market?

Free Market (internally "FMK") is a free, multi-tenant marketplace platform built for everyday sellers, service providers, and delivery riders. It's two apps in one:

- **A private back office** for business owners — stock/inventory management, sales tracking, customers, cashier accounts, receipts, and analytics for their own store.
- **A public marketplace** where anyone can browse products, message or WhatsApp a seller directly, hire a verified artisan, or book a rider — all from the same platform.

Every business gets its own **isolated** workspace: products, sales, customers, and staff never leak across businesses. Nothing to configure, nothing to pay for — it's free to join and run a store on.

## How trust works here

Free Market isn't an anonymous listing board — safety and accountability are built in:

- **Businesses are verified** before they get a public profile — ID and registration documents are captured at signup, and only confirmed businesses show up publicly.
- **Artisans and riders are tracked and reviewed.** Every completed job is an opportunity for the public to leave a rating, so good service gets recommended and bad actors get filtered out over time.
- **Riders are safety-tracked.** Live location pings back a central system while a rider is active, so deliveries stay accountable end to end — for the customer and the rider.

## Easy to find, wherever you search

You don't have to already know Free Market to find what's on it. Search a product, a service, or even a store's name on Google, and it can lead straight to that listing — no need to open the app first and search around. Location matters too: searching for something "in Lagos" or "in Rivers State" surfaces sellers, artisans, and riders local to that area.
